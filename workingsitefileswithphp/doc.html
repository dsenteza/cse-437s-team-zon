Overview and Motivation

As college students, we are often tasked with supplemental readings in some of our courses. However, the reading material is not always available online or in our WashU bookstore. We created this website as a resource to help students easily determine the closest place to rent or purchase books they may need for class or other purposes.

Related Work

We took inspiration for our project from the numerous book searching sites available for bookstores and libraries. Our website is different because it combines the stocks of multiple distributors in order to help the user narrow down the most convenient option for themselves based on location.

Objectives + Questions To Answer

We created this website to help answer the following questions:

  • Which bookstore or libraries have the particular copy in stock?
  • Which locations is the closest to the student looking for the book?


Design Evolution + Implementation

Initial Mockups

Our initial design for the website focused on isolating the important pages a user would need to navigate the page. We decided on:

  • Home Page
    • Main landing page for the site
  • Search Results Page
    • Populated the book results from the search query
    • Books can be searched by title, author, or ISBN
  • Book Details Page
    • Offer more information about the book and list off stores/libraries that copies of it are located at

Further Development

This project could be further developed to include price comparisons for each of the locations that carry a book. We couldn't get access to this information because of the scope of the project, however, price could be another factor in addition to location that could sway a consumer's decision to purchase or not from a certain vendor.
