About Us

Team Amazon is a semester-long project by Dominique Senteza, Zahra Lambe, Julia Dickerman, Lucas Florence, and Jin Hans, five Computer Science students at Washington University in St. Louis. Created for the purpose of the Software Engineering Workshop course, the team is partnering with local St. Louis bookstores to provide a one-stop book-searching platform for students, academics, and book-lovers alike.



Frequently Asked Questions:

How to I use this site?

  1. Type in your search query
  2. Select the query type that best fits your search:
    • Title
    • Keyword
    • ISBN
  3. Select the option you're looking for from the search results

What types of recommendations does the site give me?

Our website offers you the following recommendations:

  • Trending books (on the homepage under the banner)
  • Recommended school supplies (on the book details page next to the map)
  • Recommended books based off your search (on the search results page with your query and on the book details page under the book information)

Which libraries and bookstores are included in the search?

The following locations are included in our database:

  • Washington University in St. Louis Campus Store
  • Fontbonne Bookstore
  • Subterrenean Bookstore
  • Left Bank Books
  • The Wizards Wagon
